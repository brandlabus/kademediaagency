30+ Hours Saved Weekly

Lifestyle/Creator | Platform: OnlyFans/Insta

Time Freedom & Operations Hand-off

The creator was completely overwhelmed by backend operations, spending over 40 hours a week answering subscriber messages, scheduling PPV drops, and managing admin tasks. Burnout was stalling their content output and capping their earnings.

KMA Solution

  • Operational Takeover: Delegated 100% of video editing, scheduling, and project tracking to a dedicated account team.

  • Inbound Deal Flow: Setup automated brand collaboration filtering systems to instantly weed out low-paying offers.

  • Systemized Production: Introduced an optimized content pipeline allowing them to shoot a month of content in just two days.

The Key Result

By handing off backend friction, the creator reclaimed 32 hours a week. With their mental bandwidth restored, content quality skyrocketed, resulting in a completely stress-free creative workflow.

410% Monthly Revenue growth

OnlyFans & TikTok

Turning Free Fans into Paid Members

The creator had amassed over 300k social media followers but was generating less than $2,500/month. They lacked a high-converting conversion funnel and were missing out on premium revenue by relying solely on low-cost wall subscriptions.

KMA Solution

  • Traffic Funnel Architecture: Re-engineered social bios, trackable link setups, and strategic content hooks to capture high-intent leads from short-form video algorithms.

  • Premium Tier Structuring: Shifted the account strategy to a high-converting pricing matrix, focusing heavy emphasis on personalized, premium PPV sales.

  • Fan Nurturing: Installed a 24/7 engagement team to systematically interact with fans, driving high-intent leads to the subscription page.

The Key Result

Monthly earnings scaled from $2,200 to over $15,700 in under 60 days, effectively unlocking the true monetization potential of their pre-existing social audience.

2.5x Dm Retention/Sales

Entertainment | Platform: OnlyFans/Fanvue

Chatting Retainers & High-Ticket Upselling

While the creator was great at gaining new subscribers, their churn rate was incredibly high. Fans would subscribe for one month and leave because the creator couldn't keep up with personal direct messages, leading to major missed opportunities in high-ticket custom tips.

KMA Solution

  • 24/7 Chat Engagement: Integrated highly trained interaction specialists to maintain seamless, organic, round-the-clock conversation with fans across all time zones.
  • Custom Order Pipeline: Set up a structured script blueprint to pitch and lock in premium custom video sales directly within the DMs.
  • Subscriber Loyalty Funnels: Implemented automated milestone rewards and tipping triggers to keep long-term fans feeling valued and engaged.

The Key Result

Subscriber retention shot up by 140%, transforming single-month sign-ups into high-lifetime-value fans. The average revenue generated per individual subscriber more than doubled within the first quarter.
